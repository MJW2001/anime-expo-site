🎟️ Can I buy tickets at the door?

Yes, but availability is limited. We recommend buying online to secure your spot.

👜 Can I bring bags or cosplay props?

Small bags and props are allowed, but they must pass security checks.

📸 Is photography allowed inside?

Yes! But please ask for permission before photographing attendees or cosplayers.

🍔 Will food be available at the venue?

Yes. Food trucks and vendor stalls will be on-site throughout the event.
